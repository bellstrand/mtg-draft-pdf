Mechanics

Adventure

A creature card that can also be used as an instant or sorcery spell once. After using the Adventure side, the creature is exiled and may be cast at a later time.

Bargain

Bargain allows you to sacrifice an artifact, enchantment, or token as you cast the spell for an additional effect

Celebration

Celebration abilities trigger if two or more nonland permanents entered the battlefield under your control this turn.

Role Token Effects

Cursed Enchanted creature has base power and toughness 1/1.

Monster Enchanted creature gets +1/+1 and has trample.

Royal Enchanted creature gets +1/+1 and has ward {1}.

Sorcerer Enchanted creature gets +1/+1 and has "Whenever this creature attacks, scry 1."

Wicked Enchanted creature gets +1/+1 and "When this Aura is put into a graveyard from the battlefield, each opponent loses 1 life."

Young Hero Enchanted creature has "Whenever this creature attacks, if its toughness is 3 or less, put a +1/+1 counter on it."

Virtuous Enchanted creature gets +1/+1 for each enchantment you control.
