Outlaws of Thunder Junction

Archetypes

"No spells cast" plot

Plan your game and skip casting any spells on your own turn to trigger "If you haven't cast a spell from your hand this turn" abilities. This is accomplished with plot, flash and instant cards

Attrition

Scale with token creatures generation, and then sacrifice them for card draw, destroy, +1/+1 counters etc.. creatures die

Mercenary aggro

Beat your opponent with aggressive token generation, +X/0 until end of turn effects, haste and by tapping your opponents largest blockers

Mounts & vehicles

Saddle up! Tap creatures to saddle mounts or crew vehicles allowing you to charge with heavy hitters into combat. More aggressive and benefits from running more creatures and less fewer non-creature spells

Crime control

Control your opponent by committing crimes, with card advantage and ever-growing creatures. Plays a slow, grindy control game with interactive spells. Everything with "target", and instant speed to enable surprise triggers are great

Two or more spells

This is all about slinging two spells in succession. Using plot, cheap spells and draw effects to provide a lot of value

Big mana plot

Out tempo your opponent by playing multiple plot cards on early turns, while enabling big creatures and extra benefits like +1/+1 counters from using the plot mechanic

Outlaws

Utilizing outlaws, and effects that "tribal" outlaw effects. This can be super beneficial, but also complex during the drafting process since you need to keep in mind all outlaws to reap great benefits

Recursion

Graveyard play and recursion. This set provides limited mill/discard. Your focus becomes trading creatures early on and them grinding out your opponent

Ferocious

Go BIG, or go home ;) Gain benefits when attacking with a creature with power 4 or greater. Craft a plan around aggression and big bodies since the bonuses won't apply during your opponents turn

Mechanics

Plot

Plot is an alternative cost mechanic. Exile the card when paying the plot cost. On a future turn, cast the card from exile without paying its mana cost. The plot action and casting a plotted card from exile is done as a sorcery

Saddle

Saddle can only be activated as a sorcery. Similar to Crew, Saddle is activated by tapping creatures you control with total power equal to or greater then Saddle X

Crime

You commit a crime when you target your opponent, anything they control and/or cards in their graveyard

Outlaws

Outlaws include Assassins, Mercenaries, Pirates, Rogues and Warlocks. Outlaws is not considered a "creature type"

Ferocious

Gain benefits when attacking with a creature with power 4 or greater. "X, if you control a creature with power 4 or greater, Y"

Spree

Spree cards are modal spells with multiple. You must choose at least one option when casting, and each one has an additional mana cost associated with it. You can only choose each mode up to once per casting
