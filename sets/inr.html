Archetypes

Spirits

Call upon Innistrad's ethereal Spirits to evade, befuddle, and stay ahead of your earthly foes

Zombies

Control the battlefield with hordes of Zombies that also double as material components for your dark magic spells

Vampires

Combine discard outlets and madness spells to feast on blood with your Vampire army

Werewolves

Transform your Werewolves and keep them howling with instants, flash creatures, and mana-activated abilities

Humans

Humans need to be adaptable to survive! Stay ready for any situation, whether it's going wide with tokens or building tall with Equipment

Attrition

Outlast your opponents with powerful sacrifice effects, incremental damage, and plenty of willing fodder

Spells

The only suitable solution to the myriad problems on Innistrad is the endless barrage of instants and sorceries

Morbid

Attack aggressively on your turn for death-triggers, and if your opponent won't cooperate, there are always less savory methods found in the cemetery

Aggro

Leverage the most aggressive threats this plane has to offer and overrun your opponents well before their plans take effect

Self-Mill

Empower your cards with self-mill in addition to trading in combat-every creature counts, either in play or in the graveyard

Mechanics

Transform

Creatures with Transform have two sides and can transform to the other side if certain conditions are met

Disturb

Lets you cast a creature from your graveyard transformed into a Spirit or Aura

Meld

Allows two specific creatures to combine into a single, more powerful creature

Emerge

Lets you cast large Eldrazi creatures at a discount by sacrificing another creature

Madness

Allows you to cast spells for an alternative cost if you discard them

Escalate

Lets you pay additional costs to get additional effects from the same spell

Flashback

Lets you cast instants and sorceries from your graveyard for an additional cost

Investigate

Whenever you investigate, you put a clue token onto the battlefield. Clue tokens can be sacrificed to draw a card

Blood tokens

Artifact tokens that can be sacrificed to discard a card and then draw a card

Miracle

You may cast an instant or sorcery for its Miracle cost when you draw it if it's the first card you drew this turn

Undying

If a creature with Undying dies and had no +1/+1 counters on it, it returns to the battlefield with a +1/+1 counter on it

Soulbond

You may pair this creature with another unpaired creature when either enters. The paired creature gains extra abilities
