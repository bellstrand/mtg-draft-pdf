Lord of the Rings

Archetypes

Go-Wide Control

Azorius Flyers with a splash of “draw a second card” theme fill out the WU Lord of the Rings Draft archetype.

Human Aggro

Intended to get built low to the ground with low-cost creatures is the Red and White LTR Draft archetype featuring Éowyn, Merry, and Théoden. Using MTG keywords like Haste and First Strike, the archetype was designed to hit hard and fast.

Food Aggro

Food tokens provide a valuable life point resource in the Green and White LTR Draft archetype. The color pair also synergizes with the Ring tempts you mechanic, mana fixing, and powerful finishers.

Tokens Midrange

Utilizing the new LTR mechanic, the Rings tempts you with both creatures and spells. Also showcased as a theme are Rare legendaries who supports low-cost creature builds.

Sacrifice Midrange

The Black and Green color pair in LTR Limited sticks to its roots through reanimation and sacrifice synergies. Green is packed with Food tokens to support sacrifice themes and Rise of the Witch-king leads the reanimation charge.

Army Aggro

Urak-ahi leaders form armies to overrun opponents within the archetype. The color pair supports goblins and orcs through the Amass mechanic while having an aggressive go-wide gameplay strategy.

Army Control

Combining Blue and Black removal spells with the Amass mechanic, the UB archetype can stall until the build is ready to overtake an opponent.

Spellslinger

Players who enjoy casting spells can fully immerse themselves in the Lord of the Rings UR archetype.

Scry Midrange

Arwen and Legolas team up in the GU LTR Draft archetype, providing tempo through Scry, Reach, and scaling power.

Power Matters Aggro

The color Green contains mana ramp and color fixing while the RG archetype also uses Fight spells to maintain a board presence.

Mechanics

Tempted

Every time you’re “tempted” by The Ring, you will of course choose a ringbearer. The Ring gains power equal to the number of times it has "tempted" a player during the game.

Amass

Amass has returned from War of the Spark with a new twist; it’s always an orc army in this set.

Landcycling

Cards with this ability create Clue tokens - a colorless artefact token that can be sacrificed with a little colorless Mana in order to draw a card

Food Tokens

A Food token is an artifact token with the ability "{2}, {T}, Sacrifice this artifact: You gain 3 life." Snacking, er . . . sacrificing them can give you a quick burst of life when you need it, and you may find other uses for them as well.

Sagas

Each Saga enters the battlefield with a lore counter on it, causing the first ability—the chapter I ability—to trigger. As your first main phase begins, add another lore counter and the next chapter ability triggers.

Historic

Abilities may refer to historic permanents on the battlefield, historic cards in your graveyard, or historic spells. Knowing your history can ensure your future is one filled with victory.
