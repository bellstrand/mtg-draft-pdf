Bloomburrow

Archetypes

Soaring Birds

Build your team with strong ground creatures and leverage your agile flying Birds to buff them up and dominate the skies.

Graveyard Rats

Get strategic with your discards! Fill your graveyard to trigger powerful abilities on your sneaky Rats and overwhelm your opponent.

Painful Lizards

Embrace the chaos! Unleash aggressive Lizards with abilities that drain your opponent's life, bringing them closer to defeat.

Bunny Boom

Unleash the adorable power of Rabbits! This archetype focuses on creating a vast army of Rabbit tokens to overwhelm your opponent.

Mischievous Raccoons

Embrace the expendable nature of these furry friends! Sacrifice your Raccoons to trigger powerful effects and gain the upper hand.

Bloodthirsty Bats

Life is a resource! Use your Bats to drain your opponent's life while gaining life for yourself, creating a sustainable advantage.

Foraging Squirrels

These industrious Squirrels rummage through your library! Utilize their "forage" ability to search for specific cards and gain a strategic edge.

Bouncing Frogs

Welcome to the world of "enter" triggers and "flicker"! Play creatures, especially Frogs, that benefit from entering the battlefield and reuse their effects by bouncing them back to your hand.

Spellcasting Otters

Otters love a good show! Cast non-creature spells to trigger various abilities on your Otters, creating a synergy between spells and creatures.

Valiant Mice

Don't underestimate these tiny warriors! This archetype utilizes aggressive Mice with powerful "valiant" abilities that trigger when attacking or being blocked, creating a fast and relentless assault.

Mechanics

Gift

Adds a layer of political maneuvering. You can offer your opponent a "gift" (like a card draw) when you cast a spell. If they accept, they get the gift, but you gain a bonus effect from the spell.

Pawprints

Choose from multiple modes on a single spell. Each mode has a "pawprint" cost, and you can choose as many modes as you can afford, creating a customizable effect.

Offspring

Whenever a creature you control dies, you create an "offspring" creature token, allowing you to rebuild your forces and maintain pressure.

Forage

Resource-gathering mechanic lets you pay a cost by either exiling three cards from your graveyard or sacrificing a Food token. This fuels various abilities, often helping you find specific cards you need.

Expend

Rewards you for sacrificing your creatures. Spells and abilities with "expend" trigger when you spend a certain amount of mana on spells in a single turn, encouraging aggressive play and resource management.

Valiant

Ability that activates whenever a creature with "valiant" attacks or becomes blocked. It can trigger various effects, from dealing damage to drawing cards, making these aggressive creatures even more potent.
