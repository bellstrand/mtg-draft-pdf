Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty

Archetypes

Vehicles

Enable heavy undercosted vehicles by cheating crew costs with pilot tokens. Pilot tokens are hard to come by, so prioritize them while filling your deck with enough creatures to enable this strategy

Ninjas

Use cheap and evasive creatures to cheat out powerful Ninjas using the Ninjutsu mechanic in this fun tempo strategy

Artifact Sacrifice

Can be built as either an aggressive or value deck. Use cheap disposable artifacts and ways to sacrifice them or benefit from doing so

Modified

Aggressive strategy where you want to modify your creatures to enable various payoffs that rewards you for doing so

Enchantments

Midrange strategy that stacks up benefits from having enchantments in play and stacks up +1/+1 counters

Balance

Midrange strategy that utilizes a combination of artifact/enchantment theme and gains benefits from controlling both sides

Artifacts

Heavy artifact theme where getting cards that benefits from these is a priority. As typical Izzet it can be built either aggressive or control

Graveyard Recursion

Light graveyard theme. The set lacks a bit of mill and reanimate effects but makes up for it with very strong individual cards. So focus more on great individual cards then synergy

Samurai

Cool and flavorful tribal that works like "Exalted". Fill the battlefield with cards that buffs one create, attack alone and reap great benefits

Channel / Ramp

Loosely defined archetype that leans on a base of defensive creatures, ramp, and removal. Enables you to easy splash from other colors

Mechanics

Reconfigure

Creatures with reconfigure are unique in that they function as both creatures and equipment, but they always enter the battlefield as creatures

Modified

Modified is a mechanic that references a creature that's “enchanted, equipped, or has a counter on it”

Ninjutsu

A creature with Ninjutsu can be exchanged with an unblocked attacking creature and put directly into play from your hand

Channel

Every card with channel is basically a split card where you can choose either its basic functionality or its channel mode, which usually acts like another spell entirely
