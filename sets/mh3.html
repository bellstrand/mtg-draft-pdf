Archetypes

Energy Fliers

Play flying creatures that get stronger the more energy you have. Use them to attack your opponent or otherwise find tricky ways to get past your opponent's blockers.

Card Draw Control

Employ a variety of ways to draw additional cards, then leverage them for additional effects to dominate the battlefield.

Affinity Artifacts

Start small and go big. Use a variety of artifact synergies, including the infamous affinity, to summon powerhouses from Magic's past and future.

Eldrazi Spawn

Even the smallest Eldrazi are worthy of fear. Overwhelm your opponent with your terrifying Eldrazi horde, aided by Eldrazi Spawn tokens.

Modified Bestow

Bestow gives you the versatility to play creatures or use them as Auras to buff others. Go tall and then dare your opponent to stop you from going wide if the can weather the first storm.

Modified Sacrifice

In this unique take on a sacrifice strategy, you're going to be modifying your creatures with counters, Equipment, and Auras for long-term advantages.

Energy Midrange

Embrace your inner mad scientist! Energy gives you plenty of ways to manage different types of engine-like permanents to generate overwhelming advantage over your opponents.

Modified Adapt

Use +1/+1 counters to great effect, giving creatures additional spell-like triggers to overpower your opponent.

Energy Aggro

There's no point hoarding your excess energy here, so spend it and smash! Be aggressive and do whatever it takes with energy to keep attacking turn after turn.

Eldrazi Ramp

Generate Eldrazi Spawn and then use them to summon their much larger kin, and proceed to devour the Multiverse.

Mechanics

Kindred

Former: "Tribal". Kindred allows noncreature cards to have creature types. This doesn't make them actual creatures, but it does allow them to benefit from some typal effects. Ex. allows you to search your library for an Eldrazi card.

Umbra Armor

Former: "Totem Armor". Umbra armor appears on Aura cards. If a creature enchanted by an Aura with umbra armor would be destroyed, instead that Aura is destroyed and all damage dealt to the creature is removed.

Energy

Energy counters are a kind of counter that players can have. Most cards that give you energy counters also give you a way to spend them for various bonuses.

Bestow

You can cast a spell with bestow as an Aura. If you do, it gains enchant creature. If you choose to cast a spell with bestow as an Aura, it's not a creature spell at all. It's only an Aura enchantment spell

Modified

A creature you control is modified if it has one or more counters on it, if it's equipped, and/or if it's enchanted by an Aura.
